Music Macarons

Lose yourself in music

Endless. Customizable. Unique.

Music Macarons is a website where anyone with a computer and space on their device can create computer generated music, download, and then do what they want with it. You are able to use the Quick Create option where music is generated with preset values; easy for anyone in a rush. You also have the option to use Custom Create, where you have the chance to move around some nobs and click some buttons to influence the generation.

It is important to note that this website might, no, will most likely spit out something that isn't to everyone's liking. But hey, have fun with it. I try my best and there will be updates for both the website and the inner workings of this black magic. If it ends up sounding unbelievable (either good or bad), feel free to send it my way at circidalia@gmail.com where i'll feature both you and the music on my MusM YouTube channel.

Or spread it around the internet, either works :D
